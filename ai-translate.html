Frequently Asked Questions

What languages does AI Translate support?

AI Translate supports 19 languages including English, Chinese (Simplified/Traditional), Japanese, Korean, Spanish, French, German, Russian, Arabic, Hindi, Portuguese, Italian, Dutch, Turkish, Vietnamese, Thai, and Indonesian. It also features auto-detection for source language.

What's the difference between the three operation modes?

Translate mode converts text between different languages. Polishing mode improves clarity and fluency in the same language, making text sound more natural. Summarize mode creates concise summaries of long texts in your target language.

How does Word Mode work?

When you enter a single word, AI Translate automatically activates Word Mode, providing dictionary-style explanations including phonetic notation (IPA), multiple meanings with parts of speech, at least 3 example sentences, and detailed etymology.

Can I use my own AI model or API key?

Yes! AI Translate integrates with AI Chat's model system, allowing you to configure custom AI models including GPT-4, GPT-5, DeepSeek, or any OpenAI-compatible API with your own API keys.

Does it work offline?

No, AI Translate requires an internet connection to communicate with AI language models. The translation quality depends on the AI model you've configured (built-in models or your custom API).

How do I swap languages quickly?

Click the swap button (üîÑ) between the source and target language dropdowns to instantly exchange the two languages. This is perfect for bidirectional translation workflows.
